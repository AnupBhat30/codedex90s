Richard Feynman Learning Techniques

Discover the effective learning strategies of physicist Richard Feynman, known for his ability to simplify complex concepts.

Course Outline:

  • Introduction to Feynman's Techniques - Overview of his approach to learning.
  • Step 1: Identify the Topic - Choose a concept you want to learn.
  • Step 2: Teach It - Explain the concept in simple terms as if teaching someone else.
  • Step 3: Identify Gaps - Find areas where your understanding is lacking.
  • Step 4: Review and Simplify - Go back to the source material and refine your understanding.
  • Step 5: Repeat - Continue the process until you can explain the concept clearly.

Join us to enhance your learning skills using Feynman's proven methods!

Exploring the 90s Internet

Take a trip down memory lane and discover how the internet worked in the 90s! This course will guide you through the basics of navigating the early web, using dial-up connections, and understanding the culture of online communities.

Course Outline:

  • Introduction to the 90s Internet - Overview of how the internet emerged and evolved during the 90s.
  • Dial-Up Connections - Learn how to connect to the internet using dial-up and the sounds that came with it!
  • Web Browsers of the Era - Explore popular browsers like Netscape Navigator and Internet Explorer.
  • Basic HTML and Web Design - Understand the fundamentals of creating a simple web page.
  • Online Communities - Discover the rise of forums, chat rooms, and early social media.
  • Safety and Etiquette - Learn about online safety and the unwritten rules of 90s internet culture.

Join us to relive the excitement of the early web and learn how to navigate the internet like it's 1999!
